Music and movement classes for children ages 0-5...and the grownups who love them!®

 

"This is just one of the best classes out there to introduce music to children."


Every week in our music classes at Children of Music, babies, toddlers, preschoolers, and the grownups who love them come together to play musically.  Little ones love playing along in class, and caregivers love being a part of their children’s growth into confident music-makers.
